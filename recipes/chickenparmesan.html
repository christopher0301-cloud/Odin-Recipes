Chicken Parmesan

Description

Chicken Parmesan, also known as Chicken Parmigiana, is a beloved Italian-American dish that features breaded and fried chicken cutlets topped with marinara sauce and melted cheese, typically mozzarella and Parmesan. The dish is often served over pasta or with a side of vegetables, making it a comforting and satisfying meal. The crispy chicken, tangy tomato sauce, and gooey cheese create a delicious combination of flavors and textures that has made Chicken Parmesan a popular choice in Italian cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Eggs
  • Marinara sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. Dip the chicken breasts in beaten eggs, then coat them with breadcrumbs.
  4. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat and fry the chicken until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Place the fried chicken breasts in a baking dish and top each with marinara sauce, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  7. Serve hot, optionally over pasta or with a side of vegetables.
