Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta dish that consists of long, thin noodles made from wheat flour and water. It is typically served with a variety of sauces, such as marinara, Bolognese, or Alfredo, and can be topped with grated Parmesan cheese, fresh herbs, and other ingredients like meatballs or vegetables. Spaghetti is a versatile and beloved dish that is enjoyed worldwide for its simplicity and delicious flavors.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ground beef (optional)
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Cook the spaghetti noodles according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. If using ground beef, add it to the skillet and cook until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Add the tomato sauce to the skillet and simmer for 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti noodles. Optionally, top with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh herbs before serving.
